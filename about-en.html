Our Story

Renewable Energy Pioneers Since 2009

SolarNova Energy is a leading renewable and solar energy company in the Middle East, founded in 2009 with a clear vision to provide clean and sustainable energy solutions.

We pride ourselves on delivering comprehensive services including design, installation, and maintenance of solar and renewable energy systems for residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

We work with a team of specialized engineers and technicians who possess extensive experience and internationally certified credentials, using the latest global technologies to ensure the highest levels of efficiency and quality.

We have successfully completed over 500 projects across the Kingdom and the Gulf region, actively contributing to achieving Saudi Vision 2030 towards environmental sustainability.
