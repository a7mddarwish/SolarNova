FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

We answer your most common questions about our services and solar energy systems

How long does it take to install a solar energy system?

It depends on the system size, but on average, installation takes 3-7 days for residential systems, and 1-3 weeks for large commercial systems. We adhere to agreed timelines.

Does the system work on cloudy days?

Yes, solar panels work even on cloudy days but at lower efficiency (approximately 10-25% of sunny days). Therefore, we recommend storage systems or grid connection to ensure continuous supply.

How much will I save on my electricity bill?

On average, you can save 60-90% on your electricity bill depending on system size and consumption. We provide a detailed study showing expected savings and payback period (typically 4-7 years).

What is the equipment warranty period?

We provide 25-year warranty on solar panels, 10 years on inverters, 10 years on batteries, and 5 years on installation. All warranties are comprehensive and from the parent company.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we partner with several financing entities to provide flexible payment solutions including installments for periods up to 5 years with zero or very low interest rates.

Do solar panels require regular maintenance?

Solar panels don't require intensive maintenance, but we recommend cleaning them every 3-6 months to remove dust and dirt. We offer comprehensive annual maintenance contracts that include cleaning and regular inspection.
